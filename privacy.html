PromptReady

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Version 1.0.0

TL;DR — Plain English Summary

PromptReady works entirely inside your browser. It does not collect your data, send anything to a server, or require an account. Everything stays on your device.

01 — Overview

What PromptReady Does

PromptReady is a Chrome extension that enhances prompts you type into AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, Grok, DeepSeek) using built-in prompting frameworks. All processing is performed locally in your browser using JavaScript. No data is ever transmitted to any external server.

02 — Data Collection

We Collect Nothing

PromptReady does not collect, transmit, share, or sell any user data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, IP address, location)
  • No prompt content is read, stored, or sent anywhere outside your device
  • No browsing history, activity tracking, or keystroke logging
  • No analytics, crash reports, or telemetry of any kind
  • No third-party scripts, SDKs, or tracking pixels are included
  • No account or login is required
03 — Local Storage

What's Stored on Your Device

The extension uses Chrome's storage.local API to save your preferences and prompt history on your own device only. This includes:

  • Your selected framework and intensity level
  • Mode preference (manual or auto)
  • Per-site enable/disable toggles
  • Your prompt enhancement history (for the in-extension history panel)

This data never leaves your browser. You can clear it at any time by removing the extension or clearing Chrome extension storage.

04 — Permissions

Why We Request Certain Permissions

  • storage — to save your preferences locally on your device
  • activeTab — to detect which supported AI platform is active so the enhance button can appear
  • scripting — to inject the enhancement UI into the AI platform's input field
  • host permissions — limited to the six supported AI platforms only; required to interact with the prompt input element on each site
05 — Third Parties

No Third-Party Data Sharing

PromptReady does not share, sell, transfer, or disclose any user data to third parties under any circumstances. The extension contains no third-party code, advertising networks, or external service integrations.

06 — Children

Children's Privacy

PromptReady does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The extension collects no personal information from any user regardless of age.

07 — Changes

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in any material way, the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the extension after any change constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
